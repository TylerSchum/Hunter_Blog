Tyler Schum

Why did you choose Web Development?

I've spent most of my life working in a mechanic shop. this was not the job I wanted to be doing in five years, not even the next day. I longed to do something where I would be happy and proud of the work I've done. I needed a change in my life.

I decided to pursue a career where I could turn my hobby into a career. Web Development, and programming in general, was always a little hobby to me. I love both the problem solving side and the design side. It's truly a pleasure to get something working after completely analyzing the potential paths to completion.

I've never felt such motivation as I have since I've made this choice. I've gone from using some of my free time to code, to using all of my time. I study at any oppurtunity, not to keep up, but to satisfy my desire to know everything I can about this incredibly vast field. This field is incredible and provides so many oppurtunities in such a wide array of careers, I'm finally excited about my future.
